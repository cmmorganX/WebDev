Fun facts about Cocoa!

Did you know that cocoa:

  • grows in pods on trees?
  • pods are considered a fruit?
  • butter is a byproduct of cocoa harvesting?
  • has three varieties: Criollo, Trinitario, and Forastero?
  • is dried and fermented before roasting?
  • has to undergo a sweating process?
  • loses its shell during a process called winnowing?
  • is called a nib after the fermentation process?
  • nibs are enjoyed before or after roasting?

What's the most important answer?

Cocoa becomes delicious chocolate!
