Our Story

Photo by Edgar Castrejon on Unsplash Michel Bohmer and Sandy Thomas met while attending MSU. In 2006, they graduated and decided to open a coffee house, but not just any coffee house. They wanted somewhere students and commuters alike could stop, relax, and enjoy a really good cup of coffee. They wanted something different for the MSU campus life. Together, they created a funky destination that has great atmosphere, fresh drinks, live music, and really, really good food.
Settled in the heart of the MSU campus town, Savanna's Coffee House is a hot spot for coffee with an urban funk feel. Come on in and enjoy our freshly ground coffee, soup and sandwiches, and a tasty pastry treat or two! Have all of that and enjoy local acoustic music, winning prizes at one of our many game nights, or celebrating a great night out. There's always fun going on!

Our Beans

Our beans are roasted right here in our shop! Our drum roaster takes the beans on a small journey from green to java ready. Once they are done roasting, they have to cool before bagging to go home with you, or they can be freshly ground for your hot cup of coffee. All our coffees are made to serve and freshly roasted and ground daily. Stop by and try one of our specialities today!
