Types of Tea

GREEN TEA

Medium Modal Example

No oxidation occurs in green tea. It's fixed (also called oxidation prevention) by rolling, roasting, and drying it using steam, oven heat, or pan-frying. This yields a sweet-smelling, pleasant tea. Compared to black tea, green tea has a milder flavor. Although it is more common in the west, it is more common in eastern countries.

Medium Modal Heading

The Camellia sinensis plant, which also yields black, oolong, white, and Puerh tea, is the source of all green tea. The tea plant, Camellia sinensis, and Camellia asamica, have two main variations and numerous cultivars or subvarieties. The Camellia sinensis plant has more than a thousand distinct subvarieties.
